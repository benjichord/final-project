How the Cleaning Check System Works

Stay informed, clean efficiently, and avoid fines.

Our inspection system ensures dorms remain clean and comfortable through regular checks. Residents must be present during scheduled inspections, or all occupants will fail. A provided cleaning checklist outlines required tasks; uncompleted chores result in a fail and a reinspection three days later. Residents can hire professional cleaners at $35 per half hour to prepare for or recover from inspections. Semester- or year-long cleaning subscriptions are available to bypass inspections entirely. Missed cleaner appointments incur fines, and failed inspections cost $50 per resident, increasing to $100 after a second failure, with mandatory professional cleaning at $30 per half hour. Stay informed by checking schedules and completing chores on time!

Scheduled Inspections

  • Specific times will be posted on our website indicating when inspectors will visit your dorm.
  • Ensure someone from your apartment or dorm is present during the inspection. Absence results in an automatic fail.

Chore List and Cleaning Standards

  • A cleaning checklist will be provided, outlining the specific chores to complete.
  • Incomplete or insufficient cleaning will result in a fail and a reinspection within three days.

Hiring a Cleaner

  • If you fail the initial inspection, hire a cleaner to address missed chores before reinspection.
  • Cleaners are available at $35 per half hour.
  • Preemptively hire a cleaner to guarantee a pass.

Subscription Options

  • Semester- or year-long cleaning subscriptions are available for regular professional cleanings.
  • Subscriptions eliminate the need for inspections.

Resident Responsibilities

  • Be present during cleaner appointments to allow entry.
  • Failure to allow entry will result in fines.

Fines and Charges

  • Failed inspections cost $50 per resident.
  • Second failures increase fines to $100 per resident.
  • Mandatory professional cleaning after a second fail costs $30 per half hour.
